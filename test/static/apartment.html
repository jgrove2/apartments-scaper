About Avon Woods Apartments

Avon Woods, formerly Avon Mills, is located in desirable Avon, CT. This charming community is set back in a private setting with park-like grounds. Newly renovated modern one, two and three bedroom apartments with your heat and hot water included! Newly remodeled units to include: LVT flooring throughout, carpet in the bedrooms, upgraded kitchen with gorgeous Quartz countertops, upgraded bathrooms, stainless steel appliances, new paint, new keyless door locks, digital keypads, recessed lighting and light fixtures. Community Upgrades include: Upgraded lobbies, new front doors, new common area flooring, new paint in all common areas, new playground, new tennis court, Butterfly MX System Intercom/Video system in all lobbies. Our apartment homes offer large open floorplan layouts, sliders to deck or patio, fully appliance kitchens including dishwasher and spacious closets. Residents enjoy our seasonal pool and cabana, tennis courts, BBQ and Picnic areas and private walking trails. Elevators and on-site laundry facilities on every floor. Come see why you will love calling Avon Woods Home!

Avon Woods Apartments is an apartment community located in Hartford County and the 06001 ZIP Code. This area is served by the Avon attendance zone.

City - Avon

The vibrant, green hues of Avon's pastoral landscape will make you forget that you're only eight miles from Downtown Hartford and six miles from the University of Hartford. First settled in 1645 as part of Farmington, Avon's long history is reflected in its architecture, with historic sites like the Pine Grove School House, a museum built in 1865, and Avon Congregational Church, built in 1819. The town is also home to Avon Old Farms School, a prestigious all-boys boarding school that opened in 1927.

The Farmington River flows through Avon, and the town features several spectacular parks, including part of Talcott Mountain State Park, Horse Guard State Park, Fisher Meadows, and Found Land Park. If you choose an apartment in Avon, you'll enjoy outdoor activities like hiking, mountain biking, cross-country skiing, boating, fishing, golfing, and camping.

These cookies may be set through our site by our advertising partners. They may be used by those companies to build a profile of your interests and show you relevant adverts on other sites. They do not store directly personal information, but are based on uniquely identifying your browser and internet device. If you do not allow these cookies, you will experience less targeted advertising.
